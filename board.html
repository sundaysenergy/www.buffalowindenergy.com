Meet the Board of Advisors

Buffalo Wind Energy has elected a board of advisors comprising four community members to represent the landowners and make sure their voices are heard. The board is excited to bring a community-owned project to northern Texas and to provide an excellent economic resource for the community. The goal of Buffalo Wind Energy's board is to promote local participation and awareness of the project and to work closely with the landowners throughout every stage. They want to give those involved the opportunity to influence the project in a positive way. The board members are: David Christian, Randy Darnell, Todd Vincent and Casey Fox.
